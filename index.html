About This Database

This database maps transcriptomic neuronal clusters and gene expression patterns in the zebrafish dorsal habenula (dHb) to their predicted axonal projection targets in specific subdomains of the interpeduncular nucleus (IPN).

The predictions are based on a machine learning classifier (XGBoost) trained on transgenic line labeling data.

IPN Subdomains

Di - Dorsal IPN, subdomain i (non-cholinergic)
Dii - Dorsal IPN, subdomain ii (non-cholinergic)
I - Intermediate IPN (non-cholinergic)
Vi - Ventral IPN, subdomain i (cholinergic)
Vii - Ventral IPN, subdomain ii (cholinergic)
Viii - Ventral IPN, subdomain iii (cholinergic)

Halpern Lab Geisel School of Medicine at Dartmouth

Data: Pandey et al. (2018). Current Biology, 28(7), 1052-1065.
